Week 12 · Synthesis

Case Studies & Wrap-Up

Two complete pipelines, the whole toolbox on one page, and where to go from here.

Overview

The final week ties everything together with two end-to-end case studies — one on structured data (cleaning → feature selection → modeling → evaluation) and one on images (a CNN / transfer-learning pipeline) — followed by a course wrap-up. The goal is to watch complete workflows unfold and to see how the choices from every week fit together into a single decision process.

The two case studies
  • Case Study 1 — Structured data. A real numeric/categorical dataset run through the full pipeline, with model choices justified at each step.
  • Case Study 2 — Images. A CNN or pre-trained backbone on a small image set, with a frank discussion of trade-offs.

The whole toolbox, on one page

Every method from the semester, situated in a single map. Use it as a model-selection flowchart: start from the shape of your data and your goal, and follow the branches.

The DMML skill tree. Tabular & labeled → linear/logistic, then trees and boosting. Unlabeled → clustering + PCA. Sequential → ARIMA, then RNN/transformer. Images → CNN / transfer learning.
