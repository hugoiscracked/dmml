Week 10 · Sequences & Attention

Sequences & Transformers

From recurrence and memory to attention and the architecture behind modern AI.

Overview

We start with RNNs and LSTMs, which process sequences step by step and carry a hidden state. Then the breakthrough: attention lets a model look directly at any part of the input, and the Transformer builds entirely on it, dropping recurrence for parallelism. This is the architecture behind the large language models reshaping the field; we close by fine-tuning one.

By the end of this week you can…
  • Explain how an RNN carries state, and why LSTMs add gates.
  • Describe the attention mechanism and self-attention.
  • Sketch the Transformer encoder: QKV, multi-head attention, residuals, positional encoding.
  • Fine-tune a pre-trained transformer for text classification.
